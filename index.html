Who We Are

Pioneer Hill Seventh-day Adventist Church upholds an evangelistic approach by reaching upward, inward, and outward.

We are part of a movement called by God to share the eternal gospel of Jesus with every nation, tribe, language, and people. Our mission is preparing hearts for Jesus’ soon return. We help people discover the Bible’s life-changing truths for themselves, finding the freedom, healing, and hope that only Jesus offers.

More About Us

What We Believe
We believe in a God whose love never fails, even in a broken world. Despite the cosmic conflict that has raged since before time, God’s plan of salvation continues. Jesus is coming soon to restore our world to its original perfection. We await with joy the day when God will give us new hearts in a new earth, where we’ll live in His presence for eternity.

What We Do
Through our global network of churches, schools, hospitals, and humanitarian agencies, we strive to create glimpses of God’s coming kingdom in our present world. Following Jesus’ example, we serve others with compassion and love, finding Him in the faces of those we help. From community outreach to global missions, we extend Christ’s healing touch to a world in need.

Pioneer Hill Seventh-day Adventist Church is located in Kanjedza Forest (Limbe), Blantyre-MW. Our name reflects our evangelical mission, all the while embracing and engaging all races, cultures, and nationalities.

Our Vision

In harmony with Bible revelation, Seventh-day Adventists see as the climax of God’s plan the restoration of all His creation to full harmony with His perfect will and righteousness.
